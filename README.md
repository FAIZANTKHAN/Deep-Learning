What is Deep Learning?
Deep learning stands at the forefront of artificial intelligence, revolutionizing industries and shaping the future of technology. At its core, deep learning involves the utilization of neural networks‚Äîa series of algorithms inspired by the structure and functioning of the human brain‚Äîto analyze vast amounts of data, extract meaningful patterns, and make informed decisions autonomously. From image recognition and natural language processing to autonomous vehicles and medical diagnostics, the applications of deep learning are limitless.
